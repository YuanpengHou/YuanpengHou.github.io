Research interests

→ Full list

  • According to Eigenstate Thermalization Hypothesis（ETH）, Which high energy eigenstate on 2D CFT can approximate black hole (thermal state) according to ETH?

    According to black hole thermodynamics, a black hole appears as a thermalized quantum state, through the AdS3/CFT2 correspondence, this state corresponds to a thermal state on CFT2. But according to ETH, for chaotic systems, when we focus on a small subsystem, a high-energy eigenstate (pure state) behaves like a thermal state. Based on this, our question is: Which specific pure state corresponds to a black hole (thermal state)? Finding this state can easily solve many problems.

    [detail]

  • Quantization of Deformed Almheiri–Polchinski (AP) model (JT Gravity)

    The deformed AP model is a generalized model after adding free parameters to the AP model. We are dealing with the canonical quantization problem of the deformed AP model. The quantum state wave function corresponding to the deformed AP is obtained by the method of Daniel Harlow and Daniel Jafferis, the deformed AP model is a more general case. It can perfectly return to the AP model by taking the limits of the parameters. We hope that the quantization of this new model will help to study AdS2/SYK Duality.

    [detail]

  • Calculation of the entropy of multiple islands in replica wormholes

    On the basis of the replica wormholes solution proposed by scholars to solve the Hawking information paradox, we use the Island formula to calculate the entropy of Hawking radiation in the case of multiple islands and to understand the contribution of it and a single island to the entropy of Hawking radiation. This work is in progress.
