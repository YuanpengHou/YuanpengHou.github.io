Research interests

  • According to Eigenstate Thermalization Hypothesis（ETH）, Which high energy eigenstate on 2D CFT can approximate black hole (thermal state) according to ETH?


    According to the black hole thermodynamics, the black hole behaves as a thermalized quantum state. Through the AdS3/CFT2 correspondence, this state corresponds to a thermalized state on CFT2. But according to ETH, for chaotic systems, when we focus on a small subsystem, a high-energy eigenstate behaves like a thermal state. Based on this, our question is: Which specific pure state behaves like a black hole (thermal state)? Finding this state can easily solve many problems.

    The first thing we think of is the primary state. From ETH: the expected value of the pure state subsystem and the expected value of the thermal ensemble subsystem are almost equal, so we want to compare specific correlation functions between the primary state and the thermalized state on CFT2 : entanglement entropy, renyi entropy, OTOC, etc. We calculated by monodromy, Zamolodchikov's recursive relation and other methods, we found that the entanglement entropy is consistent, but the renyi entropy is inconsistent, which strongly implies that the primary state is not what we are looking for. Then we use the Reparametrization mode method to compare the OTOC of the primary state with the OTOC Lyapunov exponent behavior of the black hole (thermal state). This task is in progress.


    We are also thinking and calculating whether the descendant state or their linear superposition corresponds to a black hole (thermal state).

  • Third-level null state monodromy method

    This is a problem that suddenly comes to mind when doing the first job. The monodromy method is a method of calculating Virasoro conformal blocks: in the Liouville theory, inserting a null state at level two into the correlation function, the approximate conformal blocks can be calculated according to the monodromy condition of the expected value of the null state. Our idea is to insert another null state into the correlation function. What will be the result of a null state at level three or higher level? What is the connection between them and the internal structure of Virasoro algebra, conformal blocks?

    We calculated the result of the null state at level three and found that the conformal blocks are exactly the same as the level two result. I am looking for a mathematical connection between them.

  • Quantization of Deformed Almheiri–Polchinski (AP) model (JT Gravity)

    The deformed AP model is a generalized model after adding free parameters to the AP model. We are dealing with the canonical quantization problem of the deformed AP model.

    The quantum state wave function corresponding to the deformed AP is obtained by the method of Daniel Harlow and Daniel Jafferis, the deformed AP model is a more general case. It can perfectly return to the AP model by taking the limits of the parameters. We hope that the quantization of this new model will help to study AdS2/SYK Duality.

  • Calculation of the entropy of multiple islands in replica wormholes

    On the basis of the replica wormholes solution proposed by scholars to solve the Hawking information paradox, we use the Island formula to calculate the entropy of Hawking radiation in the case of multiple islands and to understand the contribution of it and a single island to the entropy of Hawking radiation. This work is in progress.
